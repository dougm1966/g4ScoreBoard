# LEGACY Privacy Policy

## Effective Date
Last updated: December 27, 2024

## 1. Overview
This Privacy Policy explains how g4ScoreBoard ("the Software") handles data and protects your privacy when using our scoreboard software.

## 2. Data Collection
**We do not collect any personal data.** The g4ScoreBoard software operates entirely within your local browser environment.

### Local Data Storage
The Software stores data locally on your device using:
- **LocalStorage**: For game scores, settings, and preferences
- **IndexedDB**: For uploaded images (logos, sponsor ads)

### What We Store
- Game scores and player information
- Display settings and preferences
- Uploaded images for logos and advertisements
- Temporary session data

### What We Don't Store
 personal personal personal
 personal
- personal personal

## 3. Data Processing
All data processing occurs locally in your browser. No data is transmitted to external servers or third parties.

## 4. Data Security
- All data is stored locally on your device
- No network transmissions of personal data
- Browser security protections apply
- Regular browser security updates recommended

## 5. Third-Party Services
The Software does not integrate with third-party services that collect personal data.

## 6. Data Retention
- Game data persists until manually cleared
- Images remain stored until deleted
- Browser cache clearing will remove all data

## 7. Your Rights
You have complete control over your data:
- **Access**: All data is accessible through your browser
- **Modification**: Edit any stored information
- **Deletion**: Clear data through browser settings or Software interface
- **Export**: Save your data locally

## 8. Cookies and Tracking
The Software does not use cookies or tracking technologies.

## 9. Browser Storage Management
To manage your stored data:
1. **Browser Settings**: Clear site data through browser preferences
2. **Software Interface**: Use built-in data management features
3. **Developer Tools**: Access LocalStorage and IndexedDB directly

## 10. Children's Privacy
The Software does not collect personal information from children under 13.

## 11. International Data Transfers
No data is transmitted internationally as all processing is local.

## 12. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Changes will be posted in the GitHub repository.

## 13. Contact Information
For privacy-related questions, please create an issue in the GitHub repository.

## 14. Compliance
This Privacy Policy is designed to comply with:
- General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
- California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
- Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)
